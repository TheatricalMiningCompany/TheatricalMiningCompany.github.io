Directions to LeClerc Hall

College of Notre Dame of Maryland
4701 North Charles St.
Baltimore, MD 21210


From the South

 If arriving from the South (from Baltimore City), take 83 to Cold Spring Lane heading East (right). Proceed to N. Charles and take a left. The entrance to Notre Dame is just past Loyola University.

From the North

 If arriving from the North, take 83 to Northern Parkway heading East (left). Proceed to N. Charles and take a right. The entrance to Notre Dame is past Wyndhurst and Homeland. Note: there is an entrance at Homeland, but that's the back way.

Once on the campus

 Proceed up the drive bearing right, through one traffic circle to the next circle. There you will see LeClerc hall on your left. The back box theater TMC is using is in LeClerc Hall. The easiest way to find the theater is to ignore the many welcoming doors at the top of the steps, and enter a small glass door to the left of the steps. Proceed forward, and turn left. We're a few doors down. If the steps are too inviting, go for it! You'll then come into a large lobby where you will see a stairway to your left. Take the downward stairs, past the lockeroom and you'll find us. (If you get to the basketball court, you've gone too far).

Parking   map

 Note that parking on the side of the drive is possible, but please observe posted prohibitions. Spaces marked for faculty are available to the public after 5:00 PM. Limited parking is available behind LeClerc Hall (lot "1" on the map). Additional parking can be found by continuing through the stop sign to the right (lot "2"), and at the bottom of the hill (lot "3").
