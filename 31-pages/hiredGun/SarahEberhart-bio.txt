Sarah Eberhardt (Sherri) graduated from Towson University with a BS in Acting. Her most recent roles include Mia and Josie as part of the ensemble for Glass Mind Theatre's Brainstorm3, and Feste in Twelfth Night with the Greenbelt Art Center. She also pirates aboard the Fearless in the Inner Harbor as Nighthawk throughout the plundering season. She would like to thank everyone involved in Hired Gun for making this such an exciting and rewarding experience. Enjoy the show!
