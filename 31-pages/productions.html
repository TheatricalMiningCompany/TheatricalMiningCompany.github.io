Hired Gun

by Mark Scharf
directed by Stacey Bonds
July 12 - July 29, 2012
Thurs-Fri-Sat 8:00 PM, Sun 2:00 PM

Reviews

If you're a Baltimore theater person, you should go see Load Of Fun Theater's current production of Hired Gun...
-- MD Theatre Guide

Hired Gun is a great example of the best that the Baltimore Playwrights Festival can deliver.
-- Broadway World

This well-written play knows how to set up a confrontational situation that's destined to end up with a guitar being wielded as, er, a battle ax.
-- Baltimore Sun

Synopsis

Once there was the King, the Killer and the Fat Man of Rock 'n Roll. But time has left the Fat Man forgotten in the Louisiana bayou. Jesse now has the fastest and cleanest hand in rock. Jesse's a hired gun; a studio musician who chooses his projects. Dewitt can see his way back to the top with Jesse playing for him. Intrigued by the faded rockabilly star, Jesse travels to the bayou only to find out that rock 'n roll never dies – even when it should.


Passport

by Kevin Kostic
directed by Barry Feinstein
August 9 - 26 2012
Thurs-Fri-Sat 8:00 PM, Sun 2:00 PM

Reviews

A serious examination of the limits and personal costs of humanitarian aid and relief efforts. -- J. Wynn Rousuck on Maryland Morning

In this interaction between public events and private relationships ... Kostic has achieved a fine ambivalence. -- Geoffrey Himes, City Paper

... thoughtful and intensely acted ... -- Mike Giuliano, Baltimore Sun

... Kostic goes further than depicting a city in chaos—he shows the real toll Jeff’s work takes on his marriage, the effects of poverty on women, and even touches on the AIDS epidemic in Africa. -- Morgan Halvorsen, MD Theatre Guide

Synopsis

It's December 27th, 2007 in Kisumu, Kenya. People have taken to the streets to protest the questionable results of the presidential election. Jeff, a humanitarian worker burnt-out from his job and estranged from his wife, finds himself trapped in his hotel room due to the riot occurring outside his window. Hearing screams, he pulls in two Kenyan women who will forever change his life.
