Glenn Vitale bio

Glenn Vitale (John McCardle) has studied acting for over 20 years. He has trained in NYC; LA; D.C. and Baltimore. He last performed at the Bay Theatre in Annapolis in the role of Saul Kimmer in Sam Shepard's play, "True West." One of his favorite roles was the Night Clerk in Eugene O'Neill's play, 'Hughie." He has also performed in numerous independent films and television productions. Glenn is most appreciative for the opportunity to bring the role of John McCardle to life.
