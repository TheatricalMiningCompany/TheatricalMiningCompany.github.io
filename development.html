Development

Theatrical Mining Company chooses plays from submissions to the Baltimore Playwrights Festival that were not produced, but show promise. One scene from each play is worked on using real actors. The actors are lead in improvisational exercises, then rehearse and perform the scene. After the performance night, the playwrights are given the opportunity to revise, and the play receives a full reading.
Two plays are selected by the TMC board for production as part of the BPF's summer season.
