Directions to Theatrical Mining Company

@ Load of Fun Theater
120 West North Ave.
Baltimore, MD 21201


Theatrical Mining Company moved our performance space from The College of Notre Dame to the Load of Fun Theater in the downtown Arts District. The Load of Fun space enables us to nearly double the audience we can accommodate.

Our location in the Arts District assures parking, food, and, of course, adult beverages within easy walking distance of a TMC performance. Free parking is available in the parking lot behing Load of Fun (between 19th & 20th streets), as well as on the street evenings and weekends -- free food and drink is somewhat more difficult.
